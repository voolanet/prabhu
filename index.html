  About Us

Prabhu Constructions is an evolving infrastructure development company established way back in 1986. Since inception the Prabhu Constructions has focused primarily on projects in roads, building and sewerage works. Over the years, the company has undertaken challenging projects and ensured their timely completion without any compromise on quality.With the inherent strive towards high performance and standards the company has expanded into new business verticals and geographies. Our experienced workforce of engineers, supervisors, suppliers supported by a skilled labor force, has the knowledge and ability to plan, design, superviser and construct as per requirements.

Prabhu Constructions

We at Prabhu Constructions firmly believe that the strength of an organization comes from the workforce it has. We take great care of our staff in ensuring their personal and professional growth. Our workforce is periodically given on the job trainings on new technologies and developments so as to keep abreast with the dynamic market trends. In addition to our manpower we also give due care to our machine power. Our large base of machines are periodically checked and have AMC with authorised service provides of the manufacturer.

Our exceptional Man and Machine power collectively helps us in keeping up with our pledge of zero tolerance attitude towards meeting deadlines and quality. We are proud to have received many repeat orders following on from a successfully completed competitive tender.
